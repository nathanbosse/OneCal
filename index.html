ADD IT NOW...

Taking the time plan your day is a great tool for success. One Cal is designed to let you plan what you want to do, while remaining flexible to adjust for changes. You can fill your calendar with the quick add, anything you add will show up here so you can spend less time planning and more time doing what you love.

QUICK ADD VIEW

Add from your history instantly by swiping right. Press and hold to manally add it to your day.

SEARCH

Use the search bar to find anything in your history. If it’s not found, you can tap to add it and One Cal will remember it the next time.

MORE THAN

One Cal remembers more than just titles, it can save the duration, notes, and the associated calendar to make future planning a cinch.

AN UPGRADE GETS YOU...

Weather - just so you know what to wear to your activity

Commute Times - Added to your day for explicit location changes

...WORRY ABOUT IT LATER

Calendar alerts try to keep you on time in the future at the expense of your focus at the moment. With One Cal, swiping up on the bottom bar, anywhere in the app, reveals a skimmable snapshot of your day.

FOCUS ON THE NOW

The next thing asking for your time is always visible at the bottom of One Cal.

ESTIMATED DURATION

Whenever something new begins, One Cal will schedule a notification for the estimated end so you can relax and focus.

TODAY'S SNAPSHOT

The snapshot shows everything uncomplete on your calendars, color coded by label, with the start and end times.

GETTING BETTER

One Cal shows you the current duration of anything going on now and saves the real duration to your calendar. Knowing how long something actually takes can help you plan your day better.

COMPLETION AND CROSS PLATFORM

Managing multiple calendars, possibly spread across multiple accounts, can grow to be annoying. One Cal simplifies the experience, using custom labels, so you can go back to getting things done.

RECLAIM YOUR FREE TIME

Tap on any free time block to see your history, filter by your labels or search to find what you’d like to do. Then just swipe right.

MARK IT COMPLETE

Swipe anything to the right in your day to mark it complete. This will hide it from view so you can focu on what’s next.

CROSS-PLATFORM LABELS

Set default calendars for each label and see your labels synced on all of your devices.

COLORS ARE GREAT

With colored labels assigned to all of your events, you can see what your day will be like at a glance and be ready for it.

HELPING YOU PLAN FOR YOU

Things change. One Cal helps you by automatically shifting everything that's unlocked around so they are spread out and around your meetings.

DRAG AND DROP TO REORDER

You rearrange the order and One Cal will update the start and end times and sync the changes, leaving fixed events untouched.

TAP TO TIME

Tapping the colored bar at the bottom of the screen provides options to update the starting time and start a timer.

HEX COLOR SUPPORT

One Cal ships with predefined labels and colors, but you have unlimited customization by entering custom hex color codes and titles. Click here to find your colors!
