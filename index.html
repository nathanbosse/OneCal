Every morning (or the night before) you can use One Cal to prep for the day ahead.


  • See your upcoming calendar events alongside any due reminders and free time in one view.
  • Convert reminders to calendar events to block time to ensure it gets done seamlessly.
  • Checkout the smart suggestions to fill free time with today’s most important tasks, good habits, and anything else you'd like to get done.
  • Wake up with One Cal and you’ll be ready to take on the day!

One Cal enables you to turn your calendars into a holistic preview of your day.


  • Everything you add will be sit right next to any existing events on any of your calendars.
  • Custom labels let you organize events however you like, no matter what calendar they reside on.
  • Swiping up from the bottom reveals a label colored snapshot of the rest of your day.

It’s a calendar that does work for you.

  • One Cal turns your calendars into a list of things you have to personally spend time attending to.
  • It's easy to add anything you need to do that day, but aren’t 100% sure what time you’ll get to it.
  • Everything that's unfinished that you determined to be flexible will automatically spread out and shifted forward on your calendar where they fit as the day goes on.
  • When you mark anything complete, One Cal will hide it from view and update schedule instantly, synced across all your devices.
  • One Cal remembers the duration to let you quickly re-add it later when filling your day.

One Cal provides a convenient way to adjust for changing schedules.

  • Simply press and hold on anything in your day, then drag it to reorder your day.
  • All flexible items will be adjusted to reflect the new order while fixed items will remain unchanged.
  • The best part is, everything stays synced with your calendar, accessible from all of your devices.

one calendar you'll love to use

With the goal of helping you finish everything you want to accomplish each day, one cal has a few features built in to help you stay on track.

Swiping to the right allows you to mark anything as completed which are hidden from your view by default so you can focus on what you have left.

If you open the app later in the day, you can swipe to the right on the most recent one and you'll be asked if you'd like to automatically mark everything before it complete.
